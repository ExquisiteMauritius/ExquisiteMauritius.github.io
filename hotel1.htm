Dhalia Spa Resort


The location of this premier resort, is possibly the most beautiful site in Mauritius.  Seven kilometres of beach ring the peninsula and the new deluxe rooms have been built to get the benefit of the stunning sea views. Calm, clear reef-protected waters are ideal for every kind of water sport and, close off-shore is one of the most prolific marlin breeding grounds in the world. The full range of land sports includes a par 72 international golf course.

On the scenic peninsula on the island's west coast, set against the towering mountain and almost encircled by seven kilometres of beach.
