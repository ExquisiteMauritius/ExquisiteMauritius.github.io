Renaissance Hotel




Built in the style of a fisherman's village, Renaissance Hotel is much influenced by the shapes of the traditional local fishing craft - the pirogue. The main building looks like a billowing white sail, while the individually thatched bungalows also feature the distinctive angular shapes of the boat.

A comfortable superior first class hotel, Renaissance Hotel has a long white sand beach. There is a vibrant, lively atmosphere at this resort, coupled with fine cuisine and an excellent array of sporting facilities.

Renaissance Hotel is set in the most gorgeous tropical garden, fragrant with hibiscus and frangipani - just perfect!
