Ozone Hotel


Ozone stands majestically on a promontory, overlooking the tranquil turquoise of Grand Bay. Designed to 4-star standards, offering the highest quality at truly affordable rates. The hotel is ideally situated in the centre of Grand Bay, within easy walking distance of the many restaurants and boutiques this popular holiday resort has to offer.

This magnificent resort is situated on the north western coast of Mauritius. Renowned for its long stretches of white sandy beaches, sheltered from trade winds, and located halfway between Port Louis and Grand Bay, the hotel offers a wide selection of land and water sports.
